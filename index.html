What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing? ⇩

Aluminum tubing offers lightweight strength, corrosion resistance, and excellent thermal conductivity, making it ideal for a variety of industrial applications.

How is aluminum tubing produced? ⇩

Aluminum tubing is produced through extrusion processes where heated aluminum billets are forced through a die to create the desired cross-sectional profile.
